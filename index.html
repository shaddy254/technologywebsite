HISTORY OF COMPUTERS

In this topic you will be able to know the history and the start of computing in the world which started in the early 40s

FIRST GENERATION COMPUTERS

The first generation computers were the first computers to be developed in around 1940s,this computers were characterised to store data in magnetic vacuums and were of large size,this computers were charactrized by a very slow in operation but consumed alot of heat.

The main features of the first generation are −

  • Vacuum tube technology
  • Unreliable
  • Supported machine language only
  • Very costly
  • Generated a lot of heat
  • Slow input and output devicesy
  • Huge sizey
  • Need of AC
  • Non-portable
  • Consumed a lot of electricity

second generation

these are the second computers to be developed in the year 1950s they had a reduced size and were more reliable than the first generation,these computers used the the transistors as their technology.these computers had a small size than the first generation.

third generation

these computers were more reliable than the first and the second generation computers,these computers had a speed of 1,000,000 operations per second and were multitasking in nature.it is during this generation that saw the introduction of the use of intergrated circuits.

fouth GENERATION

Fourth-generation computers were introduced in the 1970’s and developed with microprocessor chip which contains a central processing unit (CPU) on a single silicon chip. They perform on the Arithmetic Logical unit and control unit of the CPU. these are the computers we use today and are multitasking in nature.

fifth generation

these are the computers that are believed to be developed from today onwards and are said to have a human like thinking capabilities,these computers are developed with the use of artificial intelligence.
